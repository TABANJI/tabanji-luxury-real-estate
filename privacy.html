Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including your name, telephone number, email address, property requirements and correspondence.

Forms, Inquiries and Property Submissions

Contact forms, property inquiries, advisor messages and property submissions may contain personal and property-related information. In this frontend version, records are stored locally on the user’s device and are not transmitted to a server.

Local Storage

The website uses browser localStorage for language, saved properties, recently viewed properties, inquiries, submissions, newsletter records and cookie preferences. Clearing browser data removes these records from that device.

Cookies and Analytics

Necessary preference storage supports site operation. Analytics and marketing categories are placeholders only; no corresponding scripts are currently enabled.

Third-Party Services

External resources may include Google Fonts, Lucide icons, Unsplash images, WhatsApp links and map-related services. Their own policies may apply when accessed.

Retention and User Rights

When backend services are introduced, retention periods and processes for access, correction, deletion and objection should be documented in accordance with applicable law.
